About

Rishabh Dubey is a 21st-century entrepreneur who, armed with a degree from Mumbai University, embarked on a journey to transform the landscape of digital marketing in India. With a fusion of creative prowess and business acumen, he founded the Ikonic Talk Show, dedicated to enhancing brand visibility for professionals and entrepreneurs globally. Recognizing the importance of a sustainable digital marketing setup, Mr. Dubey pioneered the concept of Digital Video Marketing, fostering a digital market space for brands, corporate entities, and individuals. Through his social media company, GROW MORE, and the digital video marketing company, Ikonic Talk Show, he has empowered professionals to promote their products and services. Despite the inevitable highs and lows of entrepreneurship, Mr. Dubey remains resolute in his mission to leverage digital video marketing to connect, inspire, and transform professionals and brands worldwide, drawing from his deep understanding of business operations and a commitment to making a meaningful impact.
